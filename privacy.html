Privacy

Privacy Policy

Preneed Pilot respects your privacy. This page explains what information is collected through the website, why it is collected, and how it is used.

Last updated: April 18, 2026

We collect only the information needed to respond to you, communicate with you, and improve the site.

Overview

This Privacy Policy covers information submitted through Preneed Pilot website contact forms, email signups, and general website interaction.

It applies to information you choose to provide and limited information collected automatically through analytics tools used to understand how the website is used.

Information We Collect

Preneed Pilot collects only information you choose to provide. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Funeral home or company, if provided
  • Message content submitted through a form or email

Limited non-personal information may also be collected automatically through analytics tools. This may include pages visited, time on site, general location such as city or region, and device or browser type.

How We Use Your Information

Information may be used to respond to messages or questions, communicate about Preneed Pilot, share updates about product availability, early access, or changes, understand how pre-need agents and funeral homes may use the product, and improve the website.

Email Signups and Mailchimp

Email signups may be managed using Mailchimp. When you join the email list, the information you submit may be stored and processed by Mailchimp, and emails may be sent through Mailchimp.

You can unsubscribe at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in those emails.

Analytics and Google Analytics

Preneed Pilot uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics may collect information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, general geographic region, device type, and browser type.

This information is used in an aggregated way to understand site usage and improve the website. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google's partner sites policy page.

Sharing of Information

Preneed Pilot does not sell personal information. Information is not shared except as needed to operate the site, communicate with users, and understand website usage.

Examples may include Mailchimp for email list management, Google Analytics for site analytics, and basic website infrastructure needed to keep the site available.

Data Storage and Retention

Information is stored securely and kept only as long as needed to respond to users, maintain requested communication, manage email preferences, or support normal website operations.

Cookies

The site may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. Cookies help the site operate and help Preneed Pilot understand general website usage.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Your Choices

You can choose not to submit forms, unsubscribe from emails at any time, disable cookies through your browser settings, or request removal of your information.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request removal of your information, email hello@preneedpilot.com.

Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the site develops. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Simple summary

Preneed Pilot uses contact details to reply to you, Mailchimp to manage email updates, and Google Analytics to understand site activity.
