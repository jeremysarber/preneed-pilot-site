What is Preneed Pilot?

Preneed Pilot is pre-need software for funeral home agents and teams. It helps capture notes by voice or text, organize leads and clients, and show what needs to be done today.

How do agents use it during the day?

After a call, meeting, email, seminar, inbound inquiry, or client update, the agent speaks or types what happened. Preneed Pilot organizes it and lays out the next step.

Can I use it on my phone or computer?

Yes. Preneed Pilot is designed for phone or computer use, so agents can capture updates wherever they are working.

How does it help sell more pre-need?

It shows which leads need attention, which opportunities have gone quiet, and what action should happen next, helping agents turn more leads into funded pre-need plans.

Does it send emails and texts?

No. Preneed Pilot can draft call scripts, emails, and letters, but the agent stays in control of what is sent and when.

Who is Preneed Pilot for?

It is for individual pre-need agents, funeral homes, sales managers, and teams that need clearer lead tracking and daily action.
